Title Case Tool

This tool is based on ap-style-title-case and Title libraries and applies AP/APA and The Chicago Manual of Style title case variants to the input text.

AP style is commonly used for journalism and news writing.

APA style is used in the social sciences for research papers and academic articles. The only difference being that AP style does not recommend the use of title case for newspaper headlines, but rather sentence case.

Chicago style is best for book publishing, academic writing, and detailed publications requiring comprehensive guidelines.
